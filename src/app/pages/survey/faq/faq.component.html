How to take this survey

First of all, don’t sweat it. This isn’t a test; it’s a survey. Basically, we want you to simply reflect on yourself: we’re asking questions about when, where, and how you feel most creative. So when you take the survey, don’t overthink your answers. Just be yourself. After all, everyone’s path toward creativity is different.

Conditioned behaviors VS natural preferences

We want to know your preference. For example, if you would rather do your creative work early in the morning, but due to family obligations you end up working at night, then you should answer “Early Bird,” because that’s your preferred tendency.
