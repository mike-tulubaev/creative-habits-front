Solo
Creator

Creative species with this tendency are never found with other creatures in sight.

Solo creators are often romanticized as “lone geniuses” who shun the distraction of groups. Indeed, studies have concluded that working alone often leads to better results. This is fine for someone whose creative efforts don’t require others, but there is a lot of emphasis on creative collaboration in our society, and teamwork is often a requirement in the workplace. So what is a solo creator to do? When possible, the independent creative thinker can contribute to a collaborative project by carving out time and space to work independently on discrete parts of a group project.
